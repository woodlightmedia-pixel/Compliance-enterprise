# DOCUMENT 04: INTERNATIONAL LAW, TREATIES, AND CONVENTIONS

## 1. International Labour Organization (ILO)
* **ILO Convention No. 190 (Violence and Harassment Convention):** The first international treaty to recognize the fundamental right of everyone to a world of work free from violence and harassment, explicitly including gender-based violence and systemic discrimination. It mandates that definitions protect individuals irrespective of their contractual status (including volunteers, job applicants, and individuals exercising the authority of an employer).
* **ILO Convention No. 111 (Discrimination in Employment and Occupation):** Requires ratifying nations to declare and pursue a national policy designed to promote equality of opportunity and treatment, with an explicit focus on eliminating discrimination based on race, color, sex, religion, political opinion, or national extraction.

## 2. United Nations & European Frameworks
* **Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR):** Article 1 (All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights) and Article 23 (The right to just and favorable conditions of work).
* **European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR):** Article 14 (Prohibition of Discrimination), ensuring that the enjoyment of rights and freedoms set forth in the convention shall be secured without discrimination on any ground such as sex, race, color, language, or religion.
