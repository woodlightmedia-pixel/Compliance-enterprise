# DOCUMENT 03: LOCAL GOVERNANCE & PUBLIC SECTOR CODES

## 1. Public Sector Equality Duty (PSED - Section 149)
As a public-facing framework or an organization aligned with municipal codes, we are bound by the Public Sector Equality Duty. This requires us to actively design policies that:
* **Eliminate discrimination, harassment, and victimization** and any other conduct prohibited by national equality legislation.
* **Advance equality of opportunity** between persons who share a relevant protected characteristic and persons who do not share it.
* **Foster good relations** between different groups, actively tackling prejudice and promoting understanding.

## 2. Council Code of Conduct Benchmarks
* **The Nolan Principles:** All internal interactions and grievance procedures must adhere strictly to the Seven Principles of Public Life: Selflessness, Integrity, Objectivity, Accountability, Openness, Honesty, and Leadership.
* **Community Integration:** Ensuring that workplace anti-harassment mechanisms mirror local authority cohesion targets, actively providing multi-lingual access tools and diverse, independent mediation resources.
